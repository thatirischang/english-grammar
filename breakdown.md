<h3>S-001 · Simple Present (一般现在时)</h3>
<p class="en"><span class="s" data-tag="S">Money</span> <span class="v" data-tag="V">tells</span> <span class="o" data-tag="O">a story</span>; <span class="s" data-tag="S">the dollar</span> <span class="v" data-tag="V">tells</span> <span class="o" data-tag="O">one of the longest</span>.</p>
<p class="roles"><b>主</b> = Money / the dollar，<b>谓</b> = tells / tells，<b>宾</b> = a story / one of the longest</p>
<p class="grammar"><b>Grammar:</b> Simple Present · 用于格言式陈述。两个并列分句,分号连接。</p>
<p class="zh"><span class="s" data-tag="主">金钱</span> <span class="v" data-tag="谓">讲述</span> <span class="o" data-tag="宾">一个故事</span>；<span class="s" data-tag="主">美元</span> <span class="v" data-tag="谓">讲述</span> <span class="o" data-tag="宾">其中最漫长的一个</span>。</p>
<p class="roles"><b>主</b> = 金钱 / 美元，<b>谓</b> = 讲述 / 讲述，<b>宾</b> = 一个故事 / 其中最漫长的一个</p>
</div>

<div class="bk" id="s-002">
<h3>S-002 · Historic / Narrative Present (历史现在时)</h3>
<p class="en"><span class="a" data-tag="A">The year is 1792</span>, <span class="fn" data-tag="Fn">and</span> <span class="s" data-tag="S">a young republic</span> <span class="v" data-tag="V">stamps out</span> <span class="o" data-tag="O">its first silver dollar</span> <span class="a" data-tag="A">at the Philadelphia mint</span>.</p>
<p class="roles"><b>状</b> = The year is 1792 / at the Philadelphia mint，<b>功能词</b> = and，<b>主</b> = a young republic，<b>谓</b> = stamps out，<b>宾</b> = its first silver dollar</p>
<p class="grammar"><b>Grammar:</b> Historic Present · 历史/新闻叙事中以现在时讲过去事件,现场感强烈。</p>
<p class="zh"><span class="a" data-tag="状">时为 1792 年</span>，<span class="s" data-tag="主">年轻的共和国</span> <span class="a" data-tag="状">在费城造币厂</span> <span class="v" data-tag="谓">铸下</span> <span class="o" data-tag="宾">它的第一枚银元</span>。</p>
<p class="roles"><b>状</b> = 时为 1792 年 / 在费城造币厂，<b>主</b> = 年轻的共和国，<b>谓</b> = 铸下，<b>宾</b> = 它的第一枚银元</p>
</div>

<div class="bk" id="s-003">
<h3>S-003 · Simple Past (一般过去时)</h3>
<p class="en"><span class="a" data-tag="A">On April 2 of that year</span>, <span class="s" data-tag="S">Alexander Hamilton</span> <span class="v" data-tag="V">signed</span> <span class="o" data-tag="O">the Coinage Act</span> <span class="a" data-tag="A">into law</span>.</p>
<p class="roles"><b>状</b> = On April 2 of that year / into law，<b>主</b> = Alexander Hamilton，<b>谓</b> = signed，<b>宾</b> = the Coinage Act</p>
<p class="grammar"><b>Grammar:</b> Simple Past · 叙事的基本时态,描述某个明确过去时间点上完成的动作。</p>
<p class="zh"><span class="a" data-tag="状">那年 4 月 2 日</span>，<span class="s" data-tag="主">亚历山大 · 汉密尔顿</span> <span class="v" data-tag="谓">将...签署</span> <span class="o" data-tag="宾">《铸币法案》</span> <span class="a" data-tag="状">为法律</span>。</p>
<p class="roles"><b>状</b> = 那年 4 月 2 日 / 为法律，<b>主</b> = 亚历山大 · 汉密尔顿，<b>谓</b> = 将...签署，<b>宾</b> = 《铸币法案》</p>
</div>

<div class="bk" id="s-004">
<h3>S-004 · Past Continuous (过去进行时)</h3>
<p class="en"><span class="cl" data-tag="Cl">While</span> <span class="s" data-tag="S">Hamilton</span> <span class="v" data-tag="V">was building</span> <span class="o" data-tag="O">a national bank</span>, <span class="s" data-tag="S">Thomas Jefferson</span> <span class="v" data-tag="V">was drafting</span> <span class="o" data-tag="O">the very arguments</span> <span class="m" data-tag="M">meant to one day destroy it</span>.</p>
<p class="roles"><b>从句</b> = While，<b>主</b> = Hamilton / Thomas Jefferson，<b>谓</b> = was building / was drafting，<b>宾</b> = a national bank / the very arguments，<b>修饰</b> = meant to one day destroy it</p>
<p class="grammar"><b>Grammar:</b> Past Continuous · 两个同时持续的过去动作,常以 while 引导。</p>
<p class="zh"><span class="cl" data-tag="从">当...之时</span> <span class="s" data-tag="主">汉密尔顿</span> <span class="v" data-tag="谓">在建立</span> <span class="o" data-tag="宾">国家银行</span>，<span class="s" data-tag="主">托马斯 · 杰斐逊</span> <span class="v" data-tag="谓">正在起草</span> <span class="o" data-tag="宾">那些主张</span> <span class="m" data-tag="定">日后注定将摧毁它的</span>。</p>
<p class="roles"><b>从句</b> = 当...之时，<b>主</b> = 汉密尔顿 / 托马斯 · 杰斐逊，<b>谓</b> = 在建立 / 正在起草，<b>宾</b> = 国家银行 / 那些主张，<b>修饰</b> = 日后注定将摧毁它的</p>
</div>

<div class="bk" id="s-005">
<h3>S-005 · Past Perfect (过去完成时)</h3>
<p class="en"><span class="a" data-tag="A">By 1811</span>, <span class="s" data-tag="S">Spanish reales</span> <span class="v" data-tag="V">had circulated</span> <span class="a" data-tag="A">through the thirteen colonies</span> <span class="a" data-tag="A">for nearly a century</span>.</p>
<p class="roles"><b>状</b> = By 1811 / through the thirteen colonies / for nearly a century，<b>主</b> = Spanish reales，<b>谓</b> = had circulated</p>
<p class="grammar"><b>Grammar:</b> Past Perfect · 在过去某参照点(1811)之前已发生或延续的动作。"By + 时间"是经典锚点。</p>
<p class="zh"><span class="a" data-tag="状">到 1811 年时</span>，<span class="s" data-tag="主">西班牙银币</span> <span class="a" data-tag="状">在十三殖民地</span> <span class="v" data-tag="谓">已流通了</span> <span class="a" data-tag="状">近一个世纪</span>。</p>
<p class="roles"><b>状</b> = 到 1811 年时 / 在十三殖民地 / 近一个世纪，<b>主</b> = 西班牙银币，<b>谓</b> = 已流通了</p>
</div>

<div class="bk" id="s-006">
<h3>S-006 · Past Perfect Continuous (过去完成进行时)</h3>
<p class="en"><span class="a" data-tag="A">For two decades</span>, <span class="s" data-tag="S">the Jeffersonians</span> <span class="v" data-tag="V">had been warning</span> <span class="o" data-tag="O">their countrymen</span> <span class="a" data-tag="A">against centralized finance</span>.</p>
<p class="roles"><b>状</b> = For two decades / against centralized finance，<b>主</b> = the Jeffersonians，<b>谓</b> = had been warning，<b>宾</b> = their countrymen</p>
<p class="grammar"><b>Grammar:</b> Past Perfect Continuous · 强调动作在过去某时点之前已"持续进行"了一段时间。</p>
<p class="zh"><span class="a" data-tag="状">二十年来</span>，<span class="s" data-tag="主">杰斐逊派</span> <span class="v" data-tag="谓">一直在警告</span> <span class="o" data-tag="宾">他们的同胞</span> <span class="a" data-tag="状">警惕中央集权金融</span>。</p>
<p class="roles"><b>状</b> = 二十年来 / 警惕中央集权金融，<b>主</b> = 杰斐逊派，<b>谓</b> = 一直在警告，<b>宾</b> = 他们的同胞</p>
</div>

<div class="bk" id="s-007">
<h3>S-007 · Used to (过去常做)</h3>
<p class="en"><span class="s" data-tag="S">American farmers</span> <span class="v" data-tag="V">used to carry</span> <span class="o" data-tag="O">tobacco notes, Spanish coin, and even gold dust</span> <span class="a" data-tag="A">as everyday money</span>.</p>
<p class="roles"><b>主</b> = American farmers，<b>谓</b> = used to carry，<b>宾</b> = tobacco notes, Spanish coin, and even gold dust，<b>状</b> = as everyday money</p>
<p class="grammar"><b>Grammar:</b> used to + 动词原形 · 表过去经常做但现已不再做的习惯。区别于 be used to + V-ing。</p>
<p class="zh"><span class="s" data-tag="主">美国农人</span> <span class="v" data-tag="谓">过去常携带</span> <span class="o" data-tag="宾">烟草票、西班牙硬币、甚至金粉</span> <span class="a" data-tag="状">当作日常货币</span>。</p>
<p class="roles"><b>主</b> = 美国农人，<b>谓</b> = 过去常携带，<b>宾</b> = 烟草票、西班牙硬币、甚至金粉，<b>状</b> = 当作日常货币</p>
</div>

<div class="bk" id="s-008">
<h3>S-008 · Would (Habitual Past · 过去习惯)</h3>
<p class="en"><span class="a" data-tag="A">On Saturday mornings</span>, <span class="s" data-tag="S">a country shopkeeper</span> <span class="v" data-tag="V">would shave</span> <span class="o" data-tag="O">a thin sliver</span> <span class="a" data-tag="A">off any silver dollar</span> <span class="m" data-tag="M">he did not trust</span>.</p>
<p class="roles"><b>状</b> = On Saturday mornings / off any silver dollar，<b>主</b> = a country shopkeeper，<b>谓</b> = would shave，<b>宾</b> = a thin sliver，<b>修饰</b> = he did not trust</p>
<p class="grammar"><b>Grammar:</b> would · 表过去反复发生的习惯动作。和 used to 在表"反复动作"时可换用,但 would 不能表过去状态。</p>
<p class="zh"><span class="a" data-tag="状">每逢周六上午</span>，<span class="s" data-tag="主">乡镇店主</span> <span class="v" data-tag="谓">总会削下</span> <span class="o" data-tag="宾">一片薄银屑</span> <span class="a" data-tag="状">从任何一枚银元上</span> <span class="m" data-tag="定">他不信任的</span>。</p>
<p class="roles"><b>状</b> = 每逢周六上午 / 从任何一枚银元上，<b>主</b> = 乡镇店主，<b>谓</b> = 总会削下，<b>宾</b> = 一片薄银屑，<b>修饰</b> = 他不信任的</p>
</div>

<div class="bk" id="s-009">
<h3>S-009 · Restrictive Relative + Prep Fronted (限定性定从 · 介词前置)</h3>
<p class="en"><span class="s" data-tag="S">The dollar</span> <span class="m" data-tag="M">that Hamilton designed</span> <span class="v" data-tag="V">weighed</span> <span class="c" data-tag="C">exactly 416 grains</span>, <span class="m" data-tag="M">of which 371.25 grains were pure silver</span>.</p>
<p class="roles"><b>主</b> = The dollar，<b>修饰</b> = that Hamilton designed / of which 371.25 grains were pure silver，<b>谓</b> = weighed，<b>补</b> = exactly 416 grains</p>
<p class="grammar"><b>Grammar:</b> 一句两种定从:(a) 限定性 that-clause 限定 the dollar;(b) 非限定性 of which... 介词前置,正式书面语。</p>
<p class="zh"><span class="s" data-tag="主">那枚美元</span> <span class="m" data-tag="定">汉密尔顿设计的</span> <span class="v" data-tag="谓">重</span> <span class="c" data-tag="表">恰好 416 格令</span>，<span class="m" data-tag="补">其中 371.25 格令是纯银</span>。</p>
<p class="roles"><b>主</b> = 那枚美元，<b>修饰</b> = 汉密尔顿设计的 / 其中 371.25 格令是纯银，<b>谓</b> = 重，<b>补</b> = 恰好 416 格令</p>
</div>

<div class="bk" id="s-010">
<h3>S-010 · Non-restrictive Relative + as ... as (非限定性定从 + 同级比较)</h3>
<p class="en"><span class="s" data-tag="S">Andrew Jackson</span>, <span class="m" data-tag="M">who distrusted bankers as deeply as he distrusted aristocrats</span>, <span class="v" data-tag="V">vetoed</span> <span class="o" data-tag="O">the Second Bank's charter</span> <span class="a" data-tag="A">in 1832</span>.</p>
<p class="roles"><b>主</b> = Andrew Jackson，<b>修饰</b> = who distrusted bankers as deeply as he distrusted aristocrats，<b>谓</b> = vetoed，<b>宾</b> = the Second Bank's charter，<b>状</b> = in 1832</p>
<p class="grammar"><b>Grammar:</b> 非限定性定从用逗号隔开,补充信息;从句内 "as deeply as" 表程度相等。</p>
<p class="zh"><span class="s" data-tag="主">安德鲁 · 杰克逊</span>，<span class="m" data-tag="定">他对银行家的不信任与对贵族同样深切</span>，<span class="a" data-tag="状">在 1832 年</span> <span class="v" data-tag="谓">否决了</span> <span class="o" data-tag="宾">第二银行的特许状</span>。</p>
<p class="roles"><b>主</b> = 安德鲁 · 杰克逊，<b>修饰</b> = 他对银行家的不信任与对贵族同样深切，<b>状</b> = 在 1832 年，<b>谓</b> = 否决了，<b>宾</b> = 第二银行的特许状</p>
</div>

<div class="bk" id="s-011">
<h3>S-011 · Inverted Type-3 Conditional + Modal Perfect (倒装第三条件 + 情态完成)</h3>
<p class="en"><span class="cl" data-tag="Cl">Had</span> <span class="s" data-tag="S">Jackson</span> <span class="v" data-tag="V">trusted</span> <span class="o" data-tag="O">central banking</span>, <span class="s" data-tag="S">the Panic of 1837</span> <span class="v" data-tag="V">might never have torn</span> <span class="o" data-tag="O">the frontier</span> <span class="a" data-tag="A">apart</span>.</p>
<p class="roles"><b>从句</b> = Had，<b>主</b> = Jackson / the Panic of 1837，<b>谓</b> = trusted / might never have torn，<b>宾</b> = central banking / the frontier，<b>状</b> = apart</p>
<p class="grammar"><b>Grammar:</b> Type-3 conditional 与过去事实相反;If 从句倒装为 "Had + 主 + V-en";主句用 "might have + V-en" 情态完成式。</p>
<p class="zh"><span class="cl" data-tag="从">倘若</span> <span class="s" data-tag="主">杰克逊</span> <span class="v" data-tag="谓">信任过</span> <span class="o" data-tag="宾">中央银行制度</span>，<span class="s" data-tag="主">1837 年的恐慌</span> <span class="v" data-tag="谓">本不会撕裂</span> <span class="o" data-tag="宾">边疆</span> <span class="a" data-tag="状">四分五裂</span>。</p>
<p class="roles"><b>从句</b> = 倘若，<b>主</b> = 杰克逊 / 1837 年的恐慌，<b>谓</b> = 信任过 / 本不会撕裂，<b>宾</b> = 中央银行制度 / 边疆，<b>状</b> = 四分五裂</p>
</div>

<div class="bk" id="s-012">
<h3>S-012 · Cleft Sentence (分裂句 · It-cleft)</h3>
<p class="en"><span class="fn" data-tag="Fn">It was</span> <span class="s" data-tag="S">the Civil War</span>, <span class="m" data-tag="M">more than any economic doctrine</span>, <span class="cl" data-tag="Cl">that</span> <span class="a" data-tag="A">finally</span> <span class="v" data-tag="V">drove</span> <span class="o" data-tag="O">Washington</span> <span class="c" data-tag="C">to print paper money on a national scale</span>.</p>
<p class="roles"><b>功能词</b> = It was，<b>主</b> = the Civil War，<b>修饰</b> = more than any economic doctrine，<b>从句</b> = that，<b>状</b> = finally，<b>谓</b> = drove，<b>宾</b> = Washington，<b>补</b> = to print paper money on a national scale</p>
<p class="grammar"><b>Grammar:</b> 分裂句 (it-cleft):"It was + 强调成分 + that...",突出某一成分。这里强调"内战"。</p>
<p class="zh"><span class="fn" data-tag="句式">正是</span> <span class="s" data-tag="主">南北战争</span>，<span class="m" data-tag="插">而非任何经济理论</span>，<span class="a" data-tag="状">最终</span> <span class="v" data-tag="谓">迫使</span> <span class="o" data-tag="宾">华盛顿</span> <span class="c" data-tag="补">在全国范围内印制纸币</span>。</p>
<p class="roles"><b>功能词</b> = 正是，<b>主</b> = 南北战争，<b>修饰</b> = 而非任何经济理论，<b>状</b> = 最终，<b>谓</b> = 迫使，<b>宾</b> = 华盛顿，<b>补</b> = 在全国范围内印制纸币</p>
</div>

<div class="bk" id="s-013">
<h3>S-013 · Past Passive + To-infinitive of Purpose (过去时被动 + 不定式表目的)</h3>
<p class="en"><span class="s" data-tag="S">The greenback</span> <span class="v" data-tag="V">was issued</span> <span class="a" data-tag="A">in 1862</span> <span class="a" data-tag="A">to finance a war</span> <span class="m" data-tag="M">the Treasury could no longer pay for in coin</span>.</p>
<p class="roles"><b>主</b> = The greenback，<b>谓</b> = was issued，<b>状</b> = in 1862 / to finance a war，<b>修饰</b> = the Treasury could no longer pay for in coin</p>
<p class="grammar"><b>Grammar:</b> was issued = 过去被动语态(be + 过去分词);"to finance" 表目的;后跟省略 that 的接触关系从句。</p>
<p class="zh"><span class="s" data-tag="主">绿背钞</span> <span class="a" data-tag="状">在 1862 年</span> <span class="v" data-tag="谓">被发行</span>，<span class="a" data-tag="状">为支付一场战争</span> <span class="m" data-tag="定">国库已无法再以硬币维持的</span>。</p>
<p class="roles"><b>主</b> = 绿背钞，<b>状</b> = 在 1862 年 / 为支付一场战争，<b>谓</b> = 被发行，<b>修饰</b> = 国库已无法再以硬币维持的</p>
</div>

<div class="bk" id="s-014">
<h3>S-014 · Pseudo-cleft / Wh-noun Clause as Subject (假分裂句)</h3>
<p class="en"><span class="s" data-tag="S">What restored monetary order after Reconstruction</span> <span class="v" data-tag="V">was</span> <span class="c" data-tag="C">the Resumption Act of 1875</span>.</p>
<p class="roles"><b>主</b> = What restored monetary order after Reconstruction，<b>谓</b> = was，<b>补</b> = the Resumption Act of 1875</p>
<p class="grammar"><b>Grammar:</b> 假分裂句 (wh-cleft):"What ..." 整体作主语;系动词 was 等价对接强调焦点。</p>
<p class="zh"><span class="s" data-tag="主">在重建之后恢复货币秩序的</span>，<span class="v" data-tag="系">正是</span> <span class="c" data-tag="表">1875 年的《恢复硬币兑付法案》</span>。</p>
<p class="roles"><b>主</b> = 在重建之后恢复货币秩序的，<b>谓</b> = 正是，<b>补</b> = 1875 年的《恢复硬币兑付法案》</p>
</div>

<div class="bk" id="s-015">
<h3>S-015 · Gerund as Subject (动名词作主语)</h3>
<p class="en"><span class="s" data-tag="S">Returning to the gold standard</span> <span class="v" data-tag="V">seemed</span> <span class="c" data-tag="C">prudent to Eastern bankers and ruinous to Western farmers</span>.</p>

<div class="bk" id="s-036">

<div class="bk" id="s-037">
<h3>S-037 · Mandative Subjunctive in Apposition (虚拟 · 同位语)</h3>
<p class="en"><span class="s" data-tag="S">The new system</span> <span class="v" data-tag="V">rested</span> <span class="a" data-tag="A">upon a single condition</span>: <span class="m" data-tag="M">that no one ask for gold</span>.</p>

<p class="grammar"><b>Grammar:</b> 虚拟同位语从句 — 抽象名词 (condition/demand/suggestion/proposal) 后的 that 从句用动词原形 (此处 ask 而非 asks)。</p>

<div class="bk" id="s-038">
<h3>S-038 · As-If Manner Clause + were-subjunctive (虚拟过去 · as if)</h3>
<p class="en"><span class="a" data-tag="A">For two decades</span>, <span class="s" data-tag="S">the system</span> <span class="v" data-tag="V">worked</span> <span class="a" data-tag="A">smoothly</span>, <span class="a" data-tag="A">as if gold and the dollar were a single currency</span>.</p>

<p class="zh"><span class="a" data-tag="状">二十年间</span>，<span class="s" data-tag="主">这一体系</span> <span class="a" data-tag="状">顺畅地</span> <span class="v" data-tag="谓">运转</span>，<span class="a" data-tag="方式">仿佛黄金与美元本就是同一种货币</span>。</p>

<div class="bk" id="s-040">
<h3>S-040 · Past Perfect Passive (过去完成时被动)</h3>

<p class="zh"><span class="a" data-tag="状">1971 年 8 月 15 日</span>，<span class="s" data-tag="主">理查德 · 尼克松</span> <span class="v" data-tag="谓">宣布了</span> <span class="o" data-tag="宾从">后来被称为"尼克松冲击"的那件事</span>。</p>
